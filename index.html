Mike Flache

“Enabling digital growth requires the courage to challenge existing business models and move beyond the comfort of outdated success.”

Mike Flache is chair of the Digital Growth Collective, an international community of business, technology, and investment leaders building strategic partnerships across industries.... More

In addition, Mike collaborates with Fortune 500 companies and tech vendors on digital transformation and growth strategies. Over the years, he has worked with executives from Google, Amazon, Huawei, and Mercedes-Benz. He is also a partner at venture capital firms, including Scale Capital (U.S.) and OCBC Ventura (Indonesia).

Alongside his business ventures, Mike co-founded Safe Water Gardens (Singapore), a nonprofit dedicated to providing safe, affordable sanitation for rural communities.
